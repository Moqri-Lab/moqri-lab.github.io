About

Moqri Lab is a computational biomedical research group based at Harvard Medical School and Brigham and Women’s Hospital. The lab develops artificial intelligence and multi-omics approaches to better understand health trajectories, aging biology, and disease risk.

Our work brings together biomarkers, electronic health records, omics datasets, and clinical data to support earlier detection of disease and more precise approaches to prevention and treatment.

The lab is especially interested in aging-related disease, brain aging, Alzheimer’s disease, functional decline, and blood-based biomarkers that can be translated into clinically useful tools.
